About Movelinx Security and Delivery

We're a global logistics company dedicated to connecting businesses and people through efficient, reliable, and innovative shipping solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Movelinx Security and Delivery began as a small regional shipping company with a vision to transform the logistics industry through technology and customer-centric service.

What started with just three employees and a single truck has grown into a global operation with offices in 25 countries, a fleet of over 500 vehicles, and partnerships with major carriers worldwide.

Throughout our journey, we've remained committed to our founding principles: reliability, transparency, and innovation. We've continuously invested in cutting-edge technology and talented people to ensure we deliver the best possible service to our clients.

Today, Movelinx Security and Delivery is trusted by thousands of businesses, from small e-commerce shops to multinational corporations, to handle their most critical shipping needs.

Our Mission & Values

At Movelinx Security and Delivery, we're guided by a clear mission and a strong set of values that shape everything we do.

Our Mission

To connect the world through seamless logistics solutions that empower businesses to grow and thrive in the global marketplace. We aim to set new standards for reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction in the logistics industry.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted logistics partner, known for our innovative solutions, exceptional service, and commitment to sustainability. We envision a future where logistics is seamless, transparent, and environmentally responsible.

Reliability

We deliver on our promises, every time. Our customers can count on us for consistent, dependable service that meets their needs and exceeds their expectations.

Transparency

We believe in open, honest communication with our customers, partners, and employees. We provide real-time visibility into shipments and clear, upfront pricing.

Innovation

We continuously seek new ways to improve our services and operations. We embrace technology and creative thinking to solve complex logistics challenges.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the center of everything we do. We listen to their needs, anticipate their challenges, and tailor our solutions to help them succeed.

Global Perspective

We think and operate globally, with respect for local cultures and practices. We leverage our worldwide network to provide seamless international shipping solutions.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact and helping our customers do the same. We invest in eco-friendly technologies and practices throughout our operations.
